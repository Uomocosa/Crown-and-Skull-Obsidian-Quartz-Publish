2. ***Silvercraft***: *Cast ammunition or weapons with a smithy and silver*. ^silvercraft

4. ***Codex Language***: *You speak the oldest language known... a root of all*. ^codex-language
5. ***Ancestor***: *Call the phantom of long-dead kin to fight at your side*. ^ancestor
6. ***Skewering Strike***: *Roll this skill with any attack, and add the margin of success to the damage dealt*. ^skewering-strike
7. ***Bladeguard***: *Master of the parry. Add margin of success to [[Armor and Defense|DEF]], **1 round***. ^bladeguard
8. ***Bloodlines***: *You can glean many details from a sample of blood*. ^bloodlines
9. ***Squadron***: *Send out a call with this skill: `d6` allies arrive in `d4` rounds*. ^squadron
10. ***Fort Command***: *In garrisons or forts, take command with this skill*. ^fort-command
11. ***Artillery***: *You are a genius with range and aim at long distances. Use this skill with any long-range strategy to guarantee the success of such a roll*. ^artillery
12. ***Sun Keeper***: *Roll to manifest sunlight from an object of your choice*. ^sun-keeper
