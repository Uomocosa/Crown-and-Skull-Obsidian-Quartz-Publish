Things that make you unique, but also prove challenging in time... flaws! 
Each flaw earns **5 points**, **maximum 4 flaws** over a character’s lifetime.

----
### Character Flaws List
1. ***Addict***: You have a specific vice you cannot deny. If you can’t get your fix, the GM will ask for a roll vs. [[Attrition|attrition]] in some form. ^addict
2. ***Aescetic***: You frown on possessions. Never exceed **5** [[Total Invetory|equipment]]. ^aestetic
3. ***Ancient***: You are old! [[Skills#^muscle|Muscle]], [[Skills#^jump|Jump]], [[Skills#^climber|Climber]], [[Skills#^breakfall|Breakfall]], and [[Skills#^stealth|Stealth]] skills may not exceed **9**, but you earn respect for your advanced age. ^ancient
4. ***Bad Reputation***: You’ve done things... terrible things, and people know about it. You’ve done harm, broken oaths, or let someone down. ^bad-reputation
5. ***Crazy***: When faced with a difficult choice, choose randomly. ^crazy
6. ***Disorganized***: Where’d you put that? When seeking anything but your most-used items and armor, roll **9** or less on a `d20` to find it. ^disorganized
7. ***Drunkard***: Your drinking wavers between revelry and tomfoolery. Beer and wine drain your pockets and frustrate your friends. ^drunkard
8. ***Employed***: You are paid or oathsworn to a lord or employer. Disobey at your own peril! ^employed
9. ***Greedy***: Tempted by treasure, roll **6** or less on a `d20` to resist the urge. ^greedy
10. ***Grudge***: You have been wronged, and hold it against an individual or group, unjustly. When you encounter them, you behave terribly. ^grudge
11. ***Impetuous***: You are impatient, leaping into situations without planning or reservation. Only take action in [[Phases|phase]] **1** or **2**. ^impetuos
12. ***Injured***: This common flaw should be taken a few times in a character’s lifetime. An Injury brings a **-1** [[Total Invetory|maximum skill inventory]]. ^injured
13. **Just a kid**: Hey, I’m just a kid! Never exceed **6** [[Total Invetory|skills]]. ^just-a-kid
14. ***Paranoid***: What’s that? You hear that? You invent your own boogie men, and sew doubt among others. NPCs will be hesitant to trust you. ^paranoid
15. ***Phobia***: Fear of a common thing. In its presence, no roll can succeed. ^phobia
16. ***Pursued***: You have a nemesis out there... somewhere... hunting you. ^pursued
17. ***Sickly***: You were born frail. [[Skills#^survival|Survival]] and [[Skills#^resist|Resist]] skills cannot exceed **9**. ^sickly
18. ***Stubborn***: If you make up your mind, only a `d20` contest with another player or NPC will sway you. ^stubborn
19. ***Timid***: You’d rather let others lead. Only take action in [[Phases|phase]] **4** or **5**. ^timid
20. ***Unlucky***: No matter how many rabbit’s feet you carry, you have a penchant for comical mishaps and crit fail on a **16+**. ^unluky
